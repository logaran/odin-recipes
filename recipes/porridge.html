Oatmeal Porridge

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal 150g/li>
  • Milk 250ml
  • Pure cacao powder 15g
  • Dry banana slices a small bunch

Steps

  1. Poor oatmeal into a bowl
  2. Cover with the milk
  3. Let it rest for about fifteen minutes
  4. Add a spoonful of cocoa and stir
  5. Add a bunch of dry banana slices
  6. Enjoy!!
