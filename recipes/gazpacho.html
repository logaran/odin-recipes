Oatmeal Porridge

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe tomatoes peeled and chopped
  • 1 cucumber peeled and chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper chopped
  • 1 small red onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 4 cups tomato juice
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin (optional)
  • Croutons for garnish (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped tomatoes, cucumber, green bell pepper, red onion, and minced garlic.
  2. Pour in the tomato juice and red wine vinegar, and mix well.
  3. Slowly drizzle in the extra-virgin olive oil while stirring continuously.
  4. Season the mixture with salt, black pepper, and cumin (if desired). Adjust the seasoning to your taste.
  5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the gazpacho for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld.
  6. Before serving, give the gazpacho a good stir. If it's too thick, you can add a little more tomato juice to reach your desired consistency.
  7. Ladle the chilled gazpacho into bowls or glasses.
  8. Garnish with croutons and fresh basil leaves if you like.
  9. Serve the Gazpacho Andaluz cold and enjoy!
