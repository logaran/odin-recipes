Oatmeal Porridge

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 medium potatoes peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper or to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and thinly slice the potatoes, and finely chop the onion.
  2. In a large frying pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced potatoes and cook until they are tender but not browned, stirring occasionally. This should take about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Remove the potatoes from the pan and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  5. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Add the cooked potatoes and chopped onion to the beaten eggs, and mix well to combine.
  7. Heat a small amount of olive oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  8. Pour the egg, potato, and onion mixture into the pan and spread it out evenly.
  9. Cook the tortilla until it's set around the edges and the center is still slightly runny, about 5 minutes.
  10. Place a large plate over the pan and carefully flip the tortilla onto the plate.
  11. Slide the tortilla back into the pan and cook for an additional 3-5 minutes, or until it's completely set and golden brown on both sides.
  12. Remove the tortilla from the pan and let it cool slightly before cutting it into wedges.
  13. Serve your Spanish tortilla warm or at room temperature, and enjoy!
